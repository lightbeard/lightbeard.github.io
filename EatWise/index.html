Explore the medicinal value of food and culinary herbs

Did you know Rosemary is a preventative for memory loss?[1][2] How about that Rye is known to relieve migraine headaches?[3][4]

EatWise is a reference app for finding medicinal foods associated with health interests, conditions, and symptoms that is reference-based and easy to use. Use it to make informed decisions while grocery shopping, choosing recipes, and more. Rosemary chicken rye bread sandwich anyone?

Heavy on facts and details

Designed for the health-conscious user, EatWise provides as much relevant information as possible including condition symptoms, calories, food nutrient content, and more. And if that isn't enough, we often link to further reading — just for you — because we know you are that food nerd that stares at a phone in the grocery aisle.

Filter out the fluff with reference controls

We provide complete transparency by citing references for health conditions and medicinal food information. But what if you don't like a particular reference? No problem, EatWise allows for filtering of references so only the information you want is shown.

Empowerment through user-feedback

As big as our egos are, even we must admit EatWise is not perfect. EatWise is designed to continually improve and expand its reference database with your valuable feedback.

Study of the medicinal benefits of foods and herbs is an active area of research[5]. With new information available almost on a daily basis, EatWise is designed to stay current.

Coming soon — stay informed

EatWise is scheduled for release on Apple and Android platforms in winter 2016. Stay informed via social media or subscribe to our newsletter for a chance to participate in the beta.
