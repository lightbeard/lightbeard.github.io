ABOUT

My mother is from Honduras and my father who has Scottish/Swedish ancestry is from Quincy, Massachusetts. My father became an officer in the Navy shortly after I was born, so I spent my childhood moving almost yearly. Port cities in Veracruz, Puerto Rico, and Spain were notable homes for me among about another dozen.
