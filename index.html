Meet Robert

Robert Ross Pe­ter­son earned his stripes at IBM where as a highly acclaimed engineering talent, he developed cutting edge applications for IBM, Royal Bank of Scotland, Morgan Stanley, Macy's, Express Scripts, and the like. He later co-founded Monitoring as a Service (MaaS) startup parseix.com. He was also a key figure in the technical implementation of the Affordable Care Act (ACA), at state and federal levels, during his tenure at the Health and Human Services Commission.

Robert is the co-author of Persistence in the Enterprise, he is the inventor of fifty-eight technology patents, is a frequent conference speaker, and holds a MS in Computer Science.
