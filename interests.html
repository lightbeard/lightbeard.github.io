INTERESTS

Meditation

I've had a daily meditation practice for 4 years and have been instructing introductory meditation for about 2 years. I have interest and continue to explore a number of meditation traditions; however, my primary practice is in Heart-Rhythm Meditation and Centering Prayer.

Chess

I've always enjoyed a casual game of chess. From time to time I work on my chess game and have steadily improved to about a 1650 UCI rating. I like to play unconventional openings often discovered with my own chess app. I think my finest chess games have been against my neice who learned how to play chess with me when she was 4 years old ;)

Sailing

I take particular interest in sailing in honor of my grandfather and father's naval service. I initially learned to sail in Puerto Rico as a teenager and began my ASA certification in Lake Travis near Austin, TX. I'm excited to be out of the deserts of New Mexico and back near the ocean so I can do some more sailing!
